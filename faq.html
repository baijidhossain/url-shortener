URL Shortener FAQ

Explore these FAQs to understand our URL shortener better and enhance your online experience with efficient link management. Discover answers to common questions and learn how to make the most out of our service.

A URL shortener is a tool that converts long URLs into shorter, more manageable links. This can make sharing links easier and can help track the performance of shared URLs.
To shorten a URL, simply paste your long URL into the input field on the homepage of our URL shortener service and click the "Shorten" button. You will receive a short URL that redirects to your original link.
No, there is no limit to the number of URLs you can shorten using our service.
Yes, once a URL is shortened, it will remain active and will always redirect to the original URL.
Yes, our service provides analytics for each shortened URL. You can see the number of clicks and the referral sources.
We take data privacy seriously. Your shortened URLs and associated analytics are private and only accessible by you.
Yes, our service allows you to customize the alias of your shortened URL, making it more recognizable and brand-friendly.
We do not allow shortening URLs that link to malicious, inappropriate, or illegal content. Such URLs will be flagged and removed.
Shortened URLs are valid indefinitely, as long as they comply with our terms of service. We reserve the right to remove URLs that violate our policies.
